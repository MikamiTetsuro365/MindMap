What is Mind Map?

Simply put,about a certain thing, you can collect a thoughts and an ideas.In addition, it is convenient when you want to leave a note.

Our brain has splendid ability ”Radial thought”.In this way can connect the matter that is different to a certain matter,and widen it radially.As a result, MindMap is a tool to express a complicated thing visually.Therefore we can very easily understand complicated things.

The mind map can express the ”Radial thought” in a easily visible form!

The mind map helps you with cramming , preparation and review, and decision making. This is because the MindMap helps you with memory and understanding.
